## About Me

I am a fourth-year Ph.D. student in Economics at Georgetown University. My research interests lie in applied microeconomics with a primary focus on the economics of education and political economy. I work on topics including evaluating the college admission processes and students’ strategic responses, the value of high-schools, social interaction, and the effects of candidates’ risk perception on electoral outcomes. I use both theory and empirical methods in my [research](/research).
